Dear Wake the World Boat Captains

Spring is here and we are ready for some behind the boat time! This years event is on Monday, July 25. To confirm your participation, please RSVP now.

Boat Captains RSVP NOW

If you know other boat owners that would like to participate, please forward this too them. Remember, this event is specific to inboard wake/ski/surf boats.

We've had more captains join since the last event, so this year will be the biggest yet. Once again, we will be benefiting the Boys and Girls Club.

New Captains, welcome! Please complete a Boaters Safety online test, and send us the course certificate. This allows us to get discounted insurance for the event.

If you have previously taken the Safety online test for Wake The World, you do not need to again.

Stay tuned for more info and if you haven't already, please like our Facebook page to get WTW updates in your news feed.
