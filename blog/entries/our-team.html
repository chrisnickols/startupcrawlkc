Our Team

At the mention that startup crawl was returning to KC, several community members jumped at the chance to be part of the team including a couple of crawl veterans. Our amazing team includes Jennifer Nickols, August Patterson, Irit Gillath, John Coler, Matthew Marcus, Larissa Uredi and Seth Kakuske.

Let's hear from the team why the crawl is so important to them and for Kansas City.

"For a city to be successful, it has to have a strong sense of community. Events like the StartUp Crawl are a great way to cultivate that community and build lasting relationships between people who may never get a chance to meet otherwise. Anytime you can create a fun, focused and energetic event that brings people together, you're doing something right."
Larissa Uredi, Director of Marketing and Business Development at TheDeviceShops

"Startup Crawl is important for mending the individuals who perform in the Startup Community with the curious and potential role players of the future in Kansas City and globally. I made lasting connections and further solidified my future opportunities with a startup that I later worked for in the Kansas City Startup Village as well as friendships that I have to this day. The benefits from such an experience are immeasurable but very positive for all parties involved."
John Coler, Quality Assurance Engineer at Pinsight Media

"The startup crawl was one of the first startup events I attended after moving to KC. It was such a fantastic night and instantly made me feel connected with everything going on in the community. We met some amazing people and made some lasting connections."
Jennifer Nickols, Co-Founder Whobaloo & Lead Designer at Sherpa Innovation

"After working in the corporate world for several years, I decided to go out on my own. Attending the startup crawl was my first adventure in the startup culture in KC. It was an amazing event and after party was especially fantastic for people curious about what's going on with KC entrepreneurs."
August Patterson, CEO Sherpa Innovation
