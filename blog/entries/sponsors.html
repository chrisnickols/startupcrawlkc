Kansas City Startup Foundation
The Kansas City Startup Foundation (KCSF) unifies & champions Kansas City's entrepreneur community. Our efforts foster an entrepreneurial mindset & encourage people to engage in activities that inspire innovation.
The KCSF emerged from the multi-year, volunteer efforts of Kansas City Startup Village (KCSV), which continues to help mold & change the way people think about entrepreneurship & startup communities. The entrepreneurial mindset has shifted from a solo, isolated model to one of collaboration & mutual support. Focused on the KC entrepreneurial community, we're helping to facilitate activities & relationships that lead to a quicker ramp to commercialization & monetization for entrepreneurs and startups.
